Horse

A horse can run at speeds of up to around 27-40mph which is about 43.5-64.4km/h.

  • Scientific Name: Equus caballus
  • Average Weight: 380 – 1,000 kg Adult
  • Average Lifespan: 25 – 30 years
  • Favorite food: Apple slices, carrots, and hay cubes

Horses have stronger legs. They have hard hooves on their feet so they can run around in lots of different areas. Did you know that wild horses can be many different colors? They can be grey, black, white, or bay, which is a reddish-brown color.
